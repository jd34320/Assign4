About Us

Candy Palaces is a small amusement park created by Jessica Dixon. With the assistance of some of her favorite core childhood memories she was able to bring the park to life. Founded in 2022, it started off as a simple idea to get people for her city back outside again after being forced in the house during covid. Now, after four years of things getting back to usual Candy Palace is prepared to go global and inivite people from all over the world to the park.
